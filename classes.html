Our Classes

Hatha Yoga

Hatha yoga is a foundational practice involving postures, breathwork, and meditation. It improves flexibility, strength, and balance. Regular practice reduces stress, enhances body awareness, and cultivates a calm mind, suitable for all levels.


Beginner Friendly

Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga is a rigorous, sequential practice with a set series of postures. It cultivates intense heat and focus. This demanding yet rewarding practice builds strength, stamina, and mental discipline, leading to purification and self-transformation.

Power Yoga

Power Yoga is a dynamic, fitness-focused practice evolved from Ashtanga. It builds strength, flexibility, and endurance. This vigorous class improves cardiovascular health, boosts metabolism, and cultivates mental focus and resilience.

Iyengar Yoga

Iyengar yoga emphasizes precise alignment using props. It develops strength, flexibility, and body awareness. This methodical approach ensures safety and accessibility, promoting therapeutic benefits and a deeper understanding of postural alignment.


Beginner Friendly

Pre-Natal Yoga

Pre-natal yoga is tailored for expectant mothers, focusing on gentle poses, breathwork, and relaxation. It supports a healthy pregnancy, eases discomfort, prepares for labor, and promotes bonding with the baby.


Beginner Friendly

Post-Natal Yoga

Post-natal yoga aids recovery after childbirth with gentle poses, breathwork, and core strengthening. It restores strength, relieves tension, supports emotional well-being, and facilitates a gradual return to activity.


Beginner Friendly

Yoga Therapy

Yoga therapy adapts yoga for specific health needs, addressing conditions like back pain, stress, and anxiety. It uses tailored practices to promote healing, manage symptoms, and enhance overall well-being.


Beginner Friendly

Meditation

Meditation is a practice of focused attention, cultivating mental clarity and emotional balance. It reduces stress, improves concentration, and fosters a sense of inner peace and self-awareness.


Beginner Friendly

Pranayam

Pranayam involves breath control techniques to regulate energy flow and calm the mind. It enhances respiratory function, reduces anxiety, and promotes vitality and mental clarity.


Beginner Friendly

Aerobics

Aerobics is a dynamic form of exercise that improves cardiovascular health through rhythmic movements. It boosts stamina, burns calories, and enhances coordination, contributing to overall fitness and well-being.

Cardio

Cardio exercise elevates heart rate and improves cardiovascular health. It enhances endurance, burns calories, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases, promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Rope Therapy

Rope therapy uses ropes for support in yoga postures. It enhances alignment, balance, and flexibility. This practice aids in deepening poses, relieving chronic pain, and improving therapeutic benefits.
